Relaxio is designed to allow users to mix and match sounds for reading, relaxing, and focusing. All sound mp3s were sourced via YouTube. The singing bowls mp3 was made available courtesy of the 33bowls monastery.

To send feedback or suggest new sounds, feel free to reach out via Twitter or my personal website. You may also submit a pull request via Github.

Significant contributions to development were made by Matthew Maxwell, mentor at Bloc.io. All logos and icons were designed by Gisele Schaaf.
